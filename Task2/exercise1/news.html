L'ENTREPRENARIAT FEMININ AU CAMEROUN
Nourane Moluh Hassana Epse Fotsing, also known as Nourane Foster, born December 11, 1987 in Cairo, Egypt, is a Cameroonian entrepreneur, politician and member of the Cameroonian Party for National Reconciliation (PCRN) in the National Assembly since March 20201,2.

She is the founder of the Nourishka brand and runs the companies Nourishka Hair, Nourishka Cosmetics and Nourishka Hotel.

Nourane Fotsing was born in 1987 in Cairo, Egypt to a Cameroonian father and an Egyptian mother. She grew up in Cairo and continued her studies there until 2002, when her family left Egypt for Cameroon4. She moved to the city of Buea where she continued her secondary education at the Bilingual High School of Buea until she obtained her baccalaureate.

In 2009, she enrolled in Law at the University of Douala5. A few years later, she left Cameroon to pursue her studies in England and obtained a Bachelor's degree in Marketing at Cambridge International School. She returned to Cameroon after graduation and worked for a few years in a multinational company in Douala before starting her own business.
